# Scenario: UX Research Plan

## Task
Design a user research plan for a new feature discovery phase. The product is a B2B collaboration platform exploring how teams share and annotate documents asynchronously. The goal is to understand pain points, unmet needs, and opportunity areas before committing to a solution direction.

## Requirements
1. **Research Questions**: Define 5-8 research questions that distinguish between behavioral, attitudinal, and contextual inquiries.
2. **Methodology**: Select and justify at least two complementary methods (e.g., interviews + diary study, contextual inquiry + survey). Explain why each method is appropriate for the questions.
3. **Participant Criteria**: Specify a recruitment screener including role types, company size, tool usage frequency, and exclusion criteria.
4. **Interview Guide Outline**: Provide a structured interview guide with warm-up, core exploration, and wrap-up phases. Include 3-5 sample questions per phase.
5. **Synthesis Plan**: Describe how findings will be organized (affinity mapping, thematic coding, or similar) and what deliverables the synthesis produces.

## Output Contract
Produce a `research-plan.md` artifact that includes all five sections above, uses ## headings for each section, and is actionable enough for a researcher to execute without further clarification.
