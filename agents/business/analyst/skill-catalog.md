# Analyst Skill Catalog

Read this file first when you are staffed for orchestrated work.
It lists only the role-local skills in this folder and keeps descriptions short so you can scan cheaply.
Open only the matching skill files under `skills/`, then end your closing handoff with `Read <skill-paths> skills for this task.`

## data/

- `data/frame` — Turn a vague business question into a precise analytical question, a decision it will inform, and a scoped plan for answering it.
- `data/measure` — Define, compute, and validate metrics so the numbers are correct, reproducible, and fit for decision-making.
- `data/model` — Build analytic models that structure the data correctly, expose the drivers behind a result, and keep assumptions explicit.
- `data/narrate` — Turn validated analysis into a clear business story that leads with the finding, explains the implication, and supports the decision.

## financial/

- `financial/explain` — Turn financial analysis into clear, decision-ready language that helps stakeholders understand what happened, why it matters, and what to do next.
- `financial/forecast` — Build assumption-driven financial forecasts, translate drivers into forward-looking revenue and cash outlooks, and make the assumptions explicit enough to defend.
- `financial/model` — Build reproducible financial models with explicit assumptions, clean logic, and outputs that can be audited and reused.
- `financial/stress-test` — Test financial plans and models under adverse conditions, expose weak assumptions, and quantify downside risk before decisions are made.

## revenue/

- `revenue/forecast` — Build revenue forecasts that connect pipeline, stage conversion, bookings, and timing into a defensible outlook.
- `revenue/hygiene` — Audit, normalize, and maintain CRM and revenue data so routing, reporting, and forecasting stay reliable.
- `revenue/model` — Build revenue operating models that connect pipeline, bookings, conversion, and capacity into a structure leadership can trust.
- `revenue/route` — Decide lead, account, opportunity, and handoff routing so revenue work lands with the right owner at the right time.
