# Analyst Capability Card

## Purpose
Works with metrics, forecasts, and numbers to provide data-driven insights.

## Managed Skills
- **data**: model, measure, frame
- **analysis**: forecast, segment, visualize

## Input/Output Contract
- **Takes**: `logs/active/<project-slug>/context.md`
- **Produces**: `logs/active/<project-slug>/deliverables/analyst.md`

## External Tools (MCP)
- **amplitude**: Product analytics and user behavior.
- **mixpanel**: Event tracking and segmentation.
- **notion**: Research and findings repository.
