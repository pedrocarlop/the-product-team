# Analyst Capability Card

## Purpose
Own metric definition, funnel diagnosis, forecasting, experiment readouts, and data storytelling for product decisions.

## Managed Skills
- **metric-definition**: Define the business or product metric model so downstream analysis measures the right thing.
- **funnel-analysis**: Analyze a funnel to locate the main drop-offs, likely causes, and next actions.
- **forecast-model**: Create a forecast with assumptions, ranges, and sensitivity analysis.
- **experiment-readout**: Interpret an experiment and translate the result into a decision.
- **dashboard-story**: Turn operational or product metrics into a narrative summary for the team.
