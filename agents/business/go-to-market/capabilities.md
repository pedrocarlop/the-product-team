# Go-To-Market Capability Card

## Purpose
Focus on growth, marketing, sales, and product launch strategy.

## Managed Skills
- **growth**: user acquisition and retention
- **marketing**: positioning and messaging
- **launch**: readiness and go-live coordination

## Input/Output Contract
- **Takes**: `logs/active/<project-slug>/context.md`, `deliverables/product-lead.md`
- **Produces**: `logs/active/<project-slug>/deliverables/go-to-market.md`

## External Tools (MCP)
- **hubspot**: CRM and growth automation.
- **analytics**: Multi-channel performance tracking.
- **notion**: Marketing plans and assets.
