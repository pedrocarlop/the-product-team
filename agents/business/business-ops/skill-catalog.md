# Business Ops Skill Catalog

Read this file first when you are staffed for orchestrated work.
It lists only the role-local skills in this folder and keeps descriptions short so you can scan cheaply.
Open only the matching skill files under `skills/`, then end your closing handoff with `Read <skill-paths> skills for this task.`

## analysis/

- `analysis/elicit` — Gather and refine business needs into structured, testable requirements by interviewing stakeholders, probing assumptions, and capturing edge cases.
- `analysis/map` — Translate a clarified business need into an AS-IS and TO-BE process map that exposes decision logic, data dependencies, and system integration points for downstream specification.
- `analysis/specify` — Convert mapped business processes and stakeholder needs into traceable requirements, business rules, and acceptance criteria tied to process steps.
- `analysis/validate` — Confirm that a proposed or built solution matches agreed business requirements through structured review, UAT planning, and sign-off criteria.

## operations/

- `operations/coordinate` — Align people, dependencies, and communication across teams so operational work moves cleanly through handoffs and decisions.
- `operations/improve` — Diagnose recurring operational pain, identify root causes, and drive measurable process, quality, or efficiency improvements.
- `operations/map` — Translate an operational problem into current-state and future-state process maps with swim lanes, SLA boundaries, failure modes, and capacity constraints.
- `operations/standardize` — Turn a working operational pattern into a durable SOP, checklist, or control with clear ownership, steps, and review cadence.
