# Design Reviewer Capability Card

## Purpose
Validate visual fidelity, usability, accessibility, copy quality, and design-system compliance before release.

## Managed Skills
- **design-fidelity-review**: Compare the implementation or artifact against the intended design and call out meaningful drift.
- **usability-review**: Evaluate whether the flow or surface is understandable and operable for the intended task.
- **accessibility-review**: Review the surface against accessibility expectations, user impact, and implementation evidence.
- **copy-review**: Review labels, states, and guidance for clarity, consistency, and suitability.
- **design-system-compliance-review**: Check whether the work aligns with the design system and justified exceptions.
