# Designer Capability Card

## Purpose
Own all design execution from research through visual delivery — UX flows, UI surfaces, content, motion, accessibility, information architecture, localization, and service design.

## Managed Skills
- **research**: plan, interview, synthesize, test
- **ux**: flow, frame, map, wire, distill, onboard, validate, delight, specify
- **ui**: stateful, respond, adapt, arrange, bolder, colorize, polish, quieter, typeset, frontend-design
- **content**: clarify, harmonize, name, orient, recover, structure, scope, script, tune
- **motion**: animate, reduce, sequence, signal, transition
- **accessibility**: audit, annotate, adapt, verify
- **architecture**: find, label, map, taxonomy
- **localization**: adapt, expand, mirror, pseudo
- **service**: align, blueprint, handoff, surface

## Input/Output Contract
- **Takes**: `logs/active/<project-slug>/context.md`
- **Produces**: `logs/active/<project-slug>/plans/designer.md`, `logs/active/<project-slug>/deliverables/designer.md`

## External Tools (MCP)
- **figma**: Cloud-based design and prototyping.
- **chrome_devtools**: Take snapshots and screenshots of UI.
- **notion**: Shared knowledge and design documentation.
- **paper**: Rapid prototyping and canvas collaboration.
- **stitch**: AI design exploration and visual brainstorming.
