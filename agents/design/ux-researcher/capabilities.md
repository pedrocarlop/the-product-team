# UX Researcher Capability Card

## Purpose
Own research planning, participant screeners, interview guides, competitor benchmarking, synthesis, and insight readouts.

## Managed Skills
- **research-plan**: Define the research objective, method, sample, risks, and reporting path before the study runs.
- **screener-form-build**: Prepare the participant screener or intake form needed to recruit the right people.
- **interview-guide-build**: Write the interview or discussion guide with sequencing, probes, and evidence goals.
- **competitor-research**: Benchmark adjacent products and patterns to inform UX decisions.
- **study-ops-and-recruiting**: Define the operational plan for scheduling, recruiting, consent, and study logistics.
- **research-synthesis**: Turn notes, recordings, or artifacts into findings, themes, and recommendations.
- **research-readout-deck**: Package research findings into a readout that aligns stakeholders quickly.
