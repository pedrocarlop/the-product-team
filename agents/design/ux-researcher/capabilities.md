# UX Researcher Capability Card

## Purpose
Own research planning, participant screeners, interview guides, competitor benchmarking, synthesis, and insight readouts.

## Managed Skills

- **research-plan**: Define the research objective, method, sample, risks, and reporting path before the study runs. Anchored to GQM and the attitudinal/behavioral × qualitative/quantitative method matrix.
- **screener-form-build**: Design participant screeners using profile modeling, quota sampling logic, and behavioral disqualifier best practices.
- **interview-guide-build**: Write semi-structured interview guides with topic maps, probe laddering, JTBD framing, and evidence goals per section.
- **competitor-research**: Benchmark adjacent products and patterns using pattern sourcing, gap analysis, and implication mapping. Anchored to competitive analysis frameworks.
- **study-ops-and-recruiting**: Build the full operational dependency model (recruiting → screening → scheduling → consent → session → incentive → closeout). Grounded in UXPA ResearchOps framework.
- **research-synthesis**: Transform raw notes, recordings, and artifacts into findings, themes, and implications using thematic analysis (Braun & Clarke), affinity diagramming, and the atomic research model.
- **research-readout-deck**: Package research into stakeholder-aligned readouts using the Pyramid Principle and situation → complication → resolution → recommendation narrative arc.

## Tool Stack

### Competitor & Benchmark Research
- **Refero** — AI-powered design reference library; primary for SaaS UI pattern benchmarking
- **Mobbin** — 300K+ searchable screenshots and flows; best-in-class breadth
- **Page Flows** — Video recordings of real user journeys; best for motion and transitions
- **UX Archive** — Curated mobile patterns by flow type
- **SimilarWeb** — Quantitative competitive intelligence (traffic, audience overlap)
- **Muzli** — Passive trend feed; secondary

### Survey & Screener Tools
- **Typeform** — Conversational screeners with conditional logic; best completion rate
- **Google Forms** — Zero-cost, integrates with Google Sheets; best for internal use
- **Tally** — Lightweight, GDPR-compliant, unlimited responses; best for lean teams
- **Lyssna** — All-in-one screener + recruitment + usability testing
- **SurveyMonkey / Qualtrics** — Enterprise-grade for longitudinal or org-standard surveys

### Participant Recruitment Platforms
- **User Interviews** — Full-service recruitment with managed scheduling
- **Prolific** — Academic-grade panel with 200+ demographic filters
- **Respondent** — 4M+ verified B2B participants for niche professional audiences
- **Ethnio** — In-product intercept recruiting; owns participant CRM + screener + scheduling
- **UserTesting** — Integrated platform with fast turnaround video feedback
- **Sprig** — In-product micro-surveys for continuous discovery
- **Maze Recruit** — Built-in panel for unmoderated prototype testing

### Interview & Session Tools
- **Lookback** — Moderated usability sessions with observation rooms and timestamped tags
- **dscout** — Mobile research and diary studies; invisible observer mode
- **Maze** — AI-first unmoderated prototype testing with click paths and heatmaps
- **Grain** — Video-first meeting recorder with clip creation and Slack integration
- **tl;dv** — AI meeting recorder; strong freemium for async clip sharing
- **Fireflies.ai** — Enterprise meeting intelligence with 70+ integrations

### Synthesis & Analysis Tools
- **Dovetail** — AI-powered research repository; auto-transcription, tagging, clustering; best-in-class
- **Looppanel** — AI auto-coding against research questions; fast synthesis for moderated interviews
- **Condens** — AI-assisted tagging + highlight reels; Dovetail alternative at lower cost
- **Aurelius** — Traceable insight repository; every insight links to source sessions and quotes
- **Miro** — Collaborative affinity diagramming and synthesis workshops
- **FigJam** — Lightweight collaborative whiteboard; best if team lives in Figma
- **Notion AI** — Flexible synthesis templates and linked databases

### Research Ops & Scheduling
- **Rally UXR** — Purpose-built ResearchOps platform: CRM, scheduling, incentives, study tracking
- **Ethnio** — Intercept + screener + scheduling + pool + incentives in one platform
- **Calendly** — Standalone scheduling with calendar sync and round-robin routing
- **Tremendous** — Global incentive delivery: 1,000+ redemption options, 200+ countries
- **GreatQuestion** — All-in-one ops: recruitment, scheduling, incentives, lightweight repository

### Readout & Presentation Tools
- **Notion** — Living research repository; findings stay discoverable post-readout
- **Pitch** — Design-forward slide deck for polished executive readouts
- **Loom** — Async narrated walkthroughs with chapters and comments
- **Confluence** — Deep Jira integration; best in engineering-led orgs on Atlassian
- **Google Slides** — Zero-friction cross-functional sharing with comment threads
- **Canva** — Branded research one-pagers and infographics without design skills
